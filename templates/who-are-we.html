Who Are We

The Vegan Dinosaur started in 2019 when Mansour, who has been vegan for 4 years, moved to Jeddah to work. After living in Jeddah, one of the big struggles he was facing was to have a nice vegan meal that wasn’t Koshari or a Salad. Because of this, he started to learn how cook from Youtube recipes along with tips from 2 hour FaceTime calls with his mom. He then started to explore with different flavors and cuisines from different regions such as West Africa, Southeast Asia and of course the Arabian Cuisine. He also enjoyed making many products and sauces from scratch such as homemade tofu and Seitan from washed flour. He then started to make his friends and co-workers try his food, where they were impressed by the taste, even tho they were not vegan. This is when the idea became clear, In this day and age, where everyone is occupied by a busy lifestyle, it is important to ensure people are eating well, getting enough nutritions and also enjoying their meals. Mansour Along with his sister Sondos (who he forced her to move in with him) have started cooking and preparing delicious meals from their home for customers with minimal impact on the environment as possible.
